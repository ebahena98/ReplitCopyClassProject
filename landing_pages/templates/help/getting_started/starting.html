Creating a ToroGrub account

Creating a ToroGrub account is quick and simple!

Create an account

To create a ToroGrub account, complete the following steps:

  • Head to the Reigster tab from the header section.
  • On the Register page, input the info you want to set up your account with the following fields:
  • Email: This option allows you to set up an account with a unique email address.
  • Username: This name will display on your public profile to other ToroGrub users and is a reflection of you or your page. You can use your real name, your business name, or a nickname. Just make sure the name you choose is appropriate and respectful to the community. Your name is a part of your reputation and can affect if buyers or sellers want to work with you.
  • Password: Input the password you'd like to secure your account with. Then, enter your password again in the second field to ensure they match.
