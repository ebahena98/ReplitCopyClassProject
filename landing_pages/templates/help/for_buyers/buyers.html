Buyers

If you're meeting a seller, be ready to pay in either cash or through your phone. When you meet, examine the food carefully. If you like the food and it is described as listed, give the seller the amount you agreed to.

Tips for buyers

  • Take a good look at the photo, description, and condition of anything you’re interested in. The photo should show the actual food for sale, not a catalog photo.
  • If anything’s unclear or you want more details, send a message the seller.
  • If you want a closer look at something before you offer, message the seller to ask them to add an additional photo to the listing. Let them know if there are particular details you’d like to see.
  • Be respectful and open to counter-offers.
  • If you find a food on ToroGrub that isn't allowed under our Posting rules, or a seller seems suspicious, report it so we can investigate.
