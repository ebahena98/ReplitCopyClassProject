Getting Paid

ToroGrub makes it easy to post, sell, and get paid for your items when you sell in person.

Cash payments for in-person sales

If you meet up with a buyer in person, our first recommendation is that you use cash as it’s the safest payment option. However, perhaps you and your fellow CSUDH student may prefer using your phone to complete the transaction. At the very least, steer clear of the following:

  • Checks (personal, certified, or cashier’s)
  • Cryptocurrency
  • Gift cards
  • Any other forms of payment
